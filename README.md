## 📊 Competitor Sales Analysis – Power BI Dashboard

This Power BI project presents a comprehensive competitor sales analysis to support informed strategic decision-making. The goal is to understand the company’s performance both internally and within the broader competitive landscape.

### 🔍 Key Areas of Analysis:

- **Internal Performance:** Tracks sales trends, top-performing products, and regional sales to assess the company’s overall health.
- **Competitive Benchmarking:** Compares the company’s performance with other manufacturers to evaluate market position and identify strengths and weaknesses.
- **Product-Level Insights:** Examines how individual products perform compared to competitor offerings, highlighting opportunities for growth and improvement.

The dashboard provides business stakeholders with clear, data-driven insights to guide pricing, marketing, and product strategy decisions.

> **Note:** This project was completed as part of a DataCamp learning course and showcases applied skills in competitor benchmarking, data storytelling, and dashboard design using Power BI.
